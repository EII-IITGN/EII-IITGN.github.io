Creating Possibilities...

admin October 25, 2018

The EII seeks to create possibilities - possibilities of seeing new things, new syncings, and uncovering new depth in the everyday.


The nucleus of EII is to develop innovation led ideation in students and collaborators of IITGn. We aim to create an environment that would mediate the route between idea formation to startup incubation. Some of the territories that we shall traverse through in this mediation include aggressive discussions, quality sharing of ideas, well researched plan augmentation, and most importantly by providing additional support in the form of an ‘Entrepreneurial Inventory’


The ‘Entrepreneurial Inventory’ (EI) aims to be a contact warehouse. We shall be instrumental in coordinated connections that focus on ideation and create a team of well-balanced cogs for the same through the EI. Some have the ideas, but not the means. Others have the means, but not the ideas. Many are good communicators whilst some prefer to communicate through an alias. The EI shall be the meeting point of all these different fingers and we shall create a perfect hand.


We are still in our nascent stage, but the metamorphosis will be fast and furious.
