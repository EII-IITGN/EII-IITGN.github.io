EnQUIZitive

Ever wondered, how, most of the CEOs can make their companies well established in the world. One of the qualities common to all of them was a charismatic and dynamic personality. If we study their journey, then we come to know that they had been taking some risky decisions from the very starting.

Team EII brings you EnQUIZitive, a small quiz where you all will be in the position of these CEOs. You will be CEOs of different companies stuck in various situations, and you have to determine the decision which would fit best with the condition of the company.

  1. The quiz will consist of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Selecting the right answer will fetch you points. There will be no negative marks.
  2. The points for all the questions will be added, and a leaderboard will be made.
  3. The top 3 individuals who get the maximum number of points will be given exciting prizes by the EII. In case of more than three individuals getting the same number of points, the individuals who submitted the form earlier will receive the prize.
  4. For every participation, your group for IITGN RISE will get 3 points. The winning group with the highest number of correct answers after the quiz will receive extra 50 points, the second group with 30 points and the third group with 10 points. The additional points will be distributed equally among the members of the groups.

The quiz will be on 2nd October 2020 at 8:00 pm. The form will be open just for 1 hour. So don’t forget the time!!!

We will also be posting sample questions on the Instagram page of EII. So follow the page to get regular updates regarding the event.

So, put on your thinking caps and be ready to become Jeff Besos or maybe Elon Musk for a couple of hours.

IPL Fantasy League IITGN

It's IPL playoff time. We hope that your team made it to the playoffs. Even if it didn't (sad CSK noises), we provide you with a chance to make this IPL memorable for you.

Team EII brings you EnQUIZitive, the IPL Fantasy league for all the playoffs matches in this year's IPL. So put on your selector's cap, think differently and get a chance to earn some bragging rights over your friends. Apart from that, there are also Amazon vouchers worth Rs 1000.

Here are some basic rules of the events:

  1. We will publish a leaderboard after each match. The top 3 teams will be displayed.
  2. You are allowed to submit one team each for all the 4 playoffs matches. However, during the formation of the leaderboard, only your highest scoring team will be considered.
  3. You can send in your team till 7:30 pm on the match days. The team's list and method to submit your team will be sent to participants once they register for the event.

All the rules regarding scoring points and team restrictions will be sent individually to the registered participants.

We will also be posting sample questions on the Instagram page of EII. So follow the page to get regular updates regarding the event.

Those would submit at least 1 team will earn 3 RISE points for their group. The first position will earn 40 points, second 25 and third will earn 15 points.

Run Your Own Shop

Have you ever wondered what it’s like to run your shop? How much hustle does it take to operate a store? From arranging the raw materials to managing finance and earning enough profit so that your shop can survive. Most importantly, can it live up to customers expectations?

Well, now that the ideation part is over. It is time to execute your ideas through “Run Your Own Shop”.

What is Run Your Own Shop?

“Run your own shop” is an initiative of EII to associate students with hands-on experience, and give a sense of understanding of what it is like to operate various tasks in a shop. It will provide students with an opportunity to hone their organisational, financial and marketing skills.

You had an idea about starting a venture but got too afraid of excessive administration work and convincing them to provide the necessary fund. Fear no more. That’s where EII comes into the picture.

What will EII do and what is EII’s aim?

This initiative intends to provide sufficient resources to enthusiastic students to move their entrepreneurial journey from the comfort of their mind into the real world. EII will assist you in every step of this journey.

What skills will you learn?

  • Financial Management
  • Marketing Skills
  • Entrepreneurial Mindset
  • People/Crowd Management

Some important points to remember

The selected team will get an opportunity to run a fully functional and operating store (can be either online or offline).

The store will be operated entirely by them to set the working hour, pricing and other features.

So, put on your thinking caps and be ready to become Jeff Besos or maybe Elon Musk for a couple of hours.

Startup Fair

Have you watched TVF Pitchers and wanted to experience it in real life? Did you ever want to connect and engage with startups? We are bringing an all in one experience for you through a Startup Fair.

Team EII brings you STARTUP FAIR, an opportunity where entrepreneurs will share their journey and also float attractive internship opportunities for IITGN students.

The startup fair aims to connect the students with the startups associated with IIEC. Provide them exposure to the startup environment, learning from real business models and increase networking for the future.

In these two days, 9 startups will share their innovative work and upcoming project opportunities for IITGN students.

The event will be held on 9th & 10th January 2021 (4pm - 5pm).
